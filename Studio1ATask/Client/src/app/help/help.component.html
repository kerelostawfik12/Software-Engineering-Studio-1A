What is this website?

This is a website for a purely fictional online store, created as part of an assessment for the Software Engineering Studio subject at the University of Technology Sydney. The items and transactions made on this store are entirely fake, despite the site itself being fully functional and integrated with the PayPal API.


How do I buy things?

Simply register a customer account, add things to your cart, and then checkout using the test PayPal account or a fake credit card. You can use a fake email address when registering if you want - you just won't receive email notifications each time you get a new message from a seller.
